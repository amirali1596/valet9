Frequently Asked Questions

What days are available for trash pickup?

We offer pickup services Monday through Friday. You can choose any 2 days that work best for your schedule during signup.

What happens if I forget to put my trash out?

If you miss your scheduled pickup, you can contact us for a same-day pickup (additional fee applies) or wait until your next scheduled service day.

What items can you move to the curb?

We can move standard household trash bags and bins to the curb. For large items or hazardous materials, please contact your local waste management service.

How do I change or cancel my service?

You can modify your service anytime through your online account or by contacting us directly. We require 7 days notice for cancellations.

What items are prohibited?

We cannot move hazardous materials, electronics, or large items like furniture. Please contact your local waste management for special disposal needs.
